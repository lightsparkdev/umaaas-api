## Payment Flow
<Steps>
  <Step title="Funding">
    Payments are funded via stored balance in fiat or crypto currencies or instant local bank transfers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fiat To Crypto Conversion">
    Funds are converted to BTC
  </Step>
  <Step title="Blockchain Payment">
    Payment is then instantly settled to the receiving locale via the Lightning Network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Crypto To Fiat Conversion">
    Funds are then converted to receiving currency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Off-ramp">
    Funds are then instantly deposited in the receiving account via local banking rails
  </Step>
</Steps>
